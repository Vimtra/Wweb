About Us

We are a venture capital business that specializes in global rollups, mergers and acquisitions, startup companies, venture capital, capital placement, funding, restructuring, and exit strategies.

We invest in startups with the ambition and potential to disrupt industries and grow at a rapid rate on a global scale. The founders, partners, and our professional network's decades of experience assist the companies in our portfolio in scaling up their growth.

Venture Capital

We provide funds to entrepreneurs and supply venture capital in a variety of forms, such as seed financing, which refers to money provided by an investor to help a business grow from a prototype to a product or service. Also accessible is early-stage financing, which enables an entrepreneur to grow a company further.

Private Equity

For working capital, growth capital, and acquisition needs, we offer Private Equity, Senior, Mezzanine, and Junior Debt. We invest private equity funds in Information technology, software, Staffing, healthcare, pharmaceutical, infrastructure and manufacturing businesses.

Capital placement

Vimtra provides operating capital, growth capital, and acquisition financing through private equity, senior, mezzanine, and junior debt.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Vimtra's team of entrepreneurs specializes in domestic and cross-border mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, company combinations, and public market takeovers. Going private transactions, special committee representations, and hostile and takeover defense advising services are among our specialties.

Startups

We invest in startups and serve as full-service incubators for their development. From Seed through Series to Convertible Notes, we participate in all stages of finance.

Business Restructuring

Businesses in challenging financial situations choose us for our disciplined approach to the transaction process and our ability to rapidly implement creative financing solutions.

We work rapidly with management to acquire a complete knowledge of the situation and the challenges that all transaction participants face. Then, while stabilizing our client's business and providing resources for future growth, we design a suite of solutions to fulfill the needs of all stakeholders.

Our goal for restructuring assignments is to minimize the inherent risk, uncertainty, and management distraction involved while maximizing value to our client. We can rapidly and efficiently conduct restructuring deals because of our long-standing ties with institutional lenders and equity investors.

Our expertise can develop and deliver restructuring activities that can produce meaningful improvements to the cash flow, profit & loss, and balance sheet by collaborating with lenders, stakeholders, and all levels of management.

Our Restructuring professionals can help stressed and distressed firms stabilize and implement a strategic, operational, and financial change process.
The goal is to help a company improve its performance and generate extraordinary and long-term value for its stakeholders.

We handle everything assignments from the small to mid-market to the largest national and international restructurings and insolvencies.

Our experience also includes any legal situations that may arise as a result of restructuring or insolvencies. On a multi-jurisdictional basis, we have extensive expertise assisting clients on an investigation, enforcement, litigation, and asset recovery.

Our typical strategy clients are $5 to $500 million revenue organizations situated in North America, Asia, the Middle East, or Australia who are looking for help with specific business difficulties.

Our business restructuring and recovery services cover all verticals, with a focus on providing insight and value based on our industry knowledge and international experience.
