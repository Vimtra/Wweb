SUBASH YAMMADA

Subash is the founder of Vimtra Ventures LLC and a serial entrepreneur. Subash has over 25 years of experience in the global mergers and acquisitions (M&A) industry, startups, private equity, venture capital, business & financial strategy, business advisory, restructuring, crisis management, corporate legal affairs, problem solving, statutory compliance, and transaction management across North America, Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and the Far East markets.

Subash has responded to the firm's partners and investors' needs by developing partnerships with technology companies and launching and investing in startups. As an investor/buyer, he has successfully completed over 45 M&A transactions. Prior to co-founding Vimtra, Subash built and spin out many successful startup information technology solutions companies.

Subash has held positions for multinational corporations in the information technology, infrastructure, real estate, banking, and entertainment industries as a C-level executive and board advisor.

Subash has successfully negotiated and implemented numerous reorganization plans, asset sales, and creditor settlements, and has extensive expertise assisting companies in financial crisis on issues such as corporate governance and financing.

Subash has extensive experience representing senior and mezzanine lenders and borrowers in a variety of distressed real estate loan scenarios, including Workouts, Restructurings, Foreclosures, Deeds in Lieu of Foreclosure, Receiverships, Prepackaged Chapter 11 cases, Cramdowns, and Contested Chapter 11 cases.
